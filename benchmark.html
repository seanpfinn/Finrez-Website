Benchmark Independent

Industry Healthcare
Deliverables Branding Brand Guidelines

A brand that simplifies life care planning — designing a clear, trustworthy identity for a complex and deeply personal service. The challenge was making something sensitive and often overwhelming feel approachable, without losing the professionalism that clients need to feel confident in their decisions.

The Challenge

Life care planning sits at the intersection of healthcare, legal, and financial services — and clients arrive already overwhelmed. They're often in the middle of a health crisis, facing decisions about a family member's long-term care, navigating systems they don't understand. The brand Benchmark needed had to do something genuinely difficult: project authority and expertise while making people feel less afraid, not more.

Brands in this space default to the same visual vocabulary — navy blues, institutional serifs, stock photography of families holding hands. None of it actually serves the client's emotional state. Benchmark wanted to be different, and they needed a brand identity that could hold that intention.

Direction & Thinking

Every visual decision was made in service of one goal: reducing cognitive load for someone in a stressful situation. The color palette was chosen for warmth and legibility simultaneously — nothing clinical, nothing cold, nothing that demands effort to read. Typography was set at a weight and scale that communicates clearly without requiring concentration. Layout decisions prioritized breathing room over information density.

The "Independent" in the name was load-bearing for us creatively. Independence — of the planner, of the client's choices, of the outcome — became the emotional through-line. The visual language was built to feel like a steady hand rather than an institution. Approachable enough to invite conversation, authoritative enough to justify the trust being placed in the service.

Outcome

A complete brand identity — wordmark, color system, typography, brand guidelines, and digital design templates — delivered ready for launch. The full brand guidelines document covers every use case the team needed: digital, print, presentation, and collateral. Of all the projects in this portfolio, Benchmark is the clearest demonstration of what it means to design for a user's emotional state rather than just their information needs.
